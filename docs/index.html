1. Overview

The Saba Energy Intelligence System is an internal enterprise platform designed to automate intelligence gathering, analysis, and reporting for the global solar and energy‑storage industry. It consolidates multi‑source data, applies AI‑driven insights, and generates professional daily reports for internal decision‑making across procurement, strategy, and operations.

The system provides:

  • Automated ingestion of industry news, WeChat articles, and price data
  • AI‑powered summarization, insights, and supply‑chain impact analysis
  • Professional PDF and HTML email generation
  • Daily automated delivery with fallback SMTP
  • Modular, maintainable, and extensible architecture

        ↓

        ↓

        ↓
Automated Delivery

3.1 URL Queue System

All external URLs enter the system through a unified queue stored in: 

data/incoming_urls.jsonl

The queue ensures:

  • Deduplication
  • Status tracking
  • Unified ingestion pipeline
  • Auditability and debugging

3.2 WeChat Link Reader

Team members paste WeChat article links into: 

wechat_links.txt

Then run:

python -m solar_briefing ingest-wechat

The system automatically validates and enqueues all links.
